Weather Moodwheel

Jul, 2024 · 1 Week
person Solo Project

Recording of the interactive 3D weather mood wheel in action

How does a sunny day feel?

Weathers shift and along with it, our emotions. Weather Moodwheel is a 3D data visualization project that gives physical form to the emotional shifts we experience with changing weather.

The Challenge

How can we visualize an internal, subjective experience? The goal was to create a data-driven representation of my classmates' emotional states across four different weather conditions: sunny, rainy, cloudy, and winter.

Weathers

☀️ Sunny | 🌧️ Rainy | ☁️ Cloudy | ❄️ Winter

Emotions

😊 Happy | 😐 Calm | 😢 Sad | 😠 Angry

From Concept to Form

Initial explorations on paper, brainstorming how to map abstract emotions onto the tangible qualities of shape and structure for each weather type. Developing a design for data visualisation: What form does 'happy' visually take? How to show shifting weather?

The final 3D models

Each piece is a data point, a specific emotional weather. Their 3D forms were built on Spline, an online 3D tool.
