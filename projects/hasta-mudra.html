Hasta Mudra

Feb, 2025 · 4 Weeks
person Solo Project

Reimagining Bharatanatyam through Virtual Reality

Six Years of Dance, Reborn in Code

With 6 years of training in the Vazhavoor style of Bharatnatyam, I've experienced how hasta mudras are not just hand poses, but narratives. This project is my bridge between a lifelong passion for classical dance and the possibilities of immersive technology.

"Research shows these codified gestures activate both the motor cortex and language centers of the brain. Hasta Mudra makes this invisible connection visible."

An Immersive Journey

Step 1: Learn

Intuitive hand-tracking allows users to learn and perform the authentic gestures themselves.

Step 2: Transform

Each gesture transforms the virtual world. Perform 'Chandrakala' to raise the moon, making the symbolic meaning tangible.

Step 3: Explore

Users explore a serene, beautifully crafted space inspired by Dravidian temple aesthetics, accompanied by Carnatic music.

Bridging Culture and Technology

Built With

Unreal Engine 5

Designed For

Cultural institutions, heritage preservation, and global audiences seeking an accessible, engaging gateway to Indian classical art.

The Path Forward

This prototype opens doors to gamified learning and interactive mythological storytelling, ensuring this ancient art form continues to evolve and captivate new generations.
