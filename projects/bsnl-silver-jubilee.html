BSNL Silver Jubilee Short

Project Overview

A commemorative video design celebrating BSNL's silver jubilee milestone. This project focused on creating a visual narrative that honors the company's 25-year journey while highlighting its technological evolution and commitment to connecting communities across India.

Design Approach

The design approach centered around the theme of connection and progress. Using a warm color palette that reflects both tradition and innovation, the video showcases BSNL's transformation from a traditional telecommunications company to a modern digital service provider.

Key Features

• Timeline visualization of BSNL's 25-year journey

• Motion graphics highlighting technological milestones

• Community-focused storytelling elements

• Brand-consistent visual identity throughout

Tools Used

Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe Illustrator

Project Impact

The commemorative video successfully captured BSNL's legacy while inspiring confidence in its future direction. It was used across multiple platforms for the silver jubilee celebrations and received positive feedback from stakeholders and the public.
