An interactive VR experience where participants' hopes for the future, aligned with the UN Sustainable Development Goals, become the glowing branches of a collaboratively grown tree.

Minding the World, Together

As designers, we dream of a better future, but these hopes often remain unspoken. Kalpavriksh creates a sacred space for these dreams to be voiced, visualized, and woven into a shared vision for global progress.

Inspired by the interconnected beauty of Avatar's Tree of Souls and the framework of the UN SDGs.

Bodystorming Process

Designed for Intuitive Wonder

The interaction was structured using the GOMS model to ensure the complex technology felt like pure magic. Every action, from selecting a sprite to recording a message, was designed to be effortless, keeping the focus on the emotional and collaborative experience.

Collective Impact

As more people participate, their voices merge. The tree transforms into a vibrant, living testament to our collective power to shape a better world, proving that everyone has something to offer for the future.
