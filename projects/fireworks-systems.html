The Central Tension

"We love bursting firecrackers, but without proper safety and awareness, they endanger people, animals, and the planet."

This led to our 'What If' questions: How could we leverage this deep-rooted cultural love, rather than fight it, to create positive change?

Avenues for Change

Intervention 1: Humorous Awareness

Using sarcasm and Puneri wit in campaigns and packaging to spark conversation and critical thinking in a non-preachy way.

Intervention 2: Interactive Education

Leveraging AR/VR for immersive safety simulations and creating transparent directories to visualize impacts and promote informed choices.

Intervention 3: Bio-Innovation

Re-engineering the product itself, drawing inspiration from exploding seed pods to create festive, beautiful, and truly eco-friendly alternatives.

Systemic Learning

This project was an exercise in moving beyond surface-level symptoms to address the root structures and beliefs of a complex system. It demonstrated that for a challenge as deeply cultural as fireworks, effective solutions must be multifaceted, empathetic, and engage the system at multiple levels simultaneously.

I learned that a system is a set of interconnected parts working toward a goal, driven by feedback loops. The outcomes are greater than the sum of its parts and are shaped by people's beliefs. Changing a system requires shifting these underlying mental models. This confirms the principle: "the cure for the system's problems is in the system itself."
