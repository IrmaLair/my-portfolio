Core Principles of Storytelling

Learning 1: Structure is Key

A clear narrative arc (Beginning, Middle, End) is fundamental for driving engagement and giving the audience a sense of purpose and direction.

Learning 2: Pacing Over Blankness

I learned that prolonged emptiness, intended as pause, can disengage the audience. Every moment needs intention, supported by visual or audio cues to maintain immersion.

The Foundation of Immersion

This project was a foundational lesson in the power of simplicity. It proved that advanced technology isn't a prerequisite for immersion; a compelling story, told with intention and collaboration, can be profoundly powerful on its own.
