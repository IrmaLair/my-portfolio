A context-aware co-pilot that transforms airport navigation through multimodal interaction.

The Empathetic XR Agent for Travel

Skye is an agentic AI for XR platform that uses conversational AI and context-awareness to guide travelers seamlessly for transport, reducing stress and cognitive load.

Reimagining Airport Mobility

Our brief was to Design user-agent interactions for a futuristic and ubiquitous agent in an XR device. We narrowed down our use case to the modern air travel which can be complex and stressful.

Intelligent Action

A modular system of specialized AI agents (for navigation, scheduling, etc.) works collaboratively to anticipate needs and act proactively on the user's behalf.

Skye in Action

Proactive Navigation

Upon landing, Skye automatically provides real-time wayfinding to baggage claim and exits, anchored to your environment for intuitive guidance.

The Agentic Camera

Senses when you want to capture a moment. It auto-frames, stabilizes, and prepares the media for easy sharing, all with minimal input.

Frictionless Booking

When you need a ride, Skye surfaces vetted options and handles the booking within the XR environment, reducing decision fatigue.

Principles of Calm Technology

The UI is designed to respect human attention. It remains passive until needed, uses progressive disclosure to avoid overload, and speaks with the tone of a reliable travel companion, not a machine.
