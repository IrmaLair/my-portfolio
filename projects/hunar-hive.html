More Than a Marketplace

Hunar Hive was conceived to bridge the gap between classroom learning and real-world entrepreneurship. It provides a dedicated space for students to showcase, sell, and scale their creations, transforming talent into tangible success.

Beyond the Brand

Designing Hunar Hive reinforced that a marketplace's true value isn't in transactions, but in the community it fosters. My key takeaway was learning to architect a brand from scratch and that the most powerful brands are built on a clear, actionable mission.
