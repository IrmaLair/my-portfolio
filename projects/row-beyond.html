The Problem & Persona: A Rower's Dilemma

DJX hack provided us a 'Sportsperson / Coach / Fitness trainee / Trainer' persona. Our teammate Janice, being a professional rower, gave us insights from her own experience and from talking with her coach and classmates.

Outdoor rowing is limited by location, safety, and weather. Indoor training is safe but monotonous and lacks real-world technique feedback.

🚫

The Solution

The Virtual River

A storyboard of our VR simulation

Row Beyond creates a secure, lifelike training environment. It uses motion tracking to monitor your grip and posture, providing real-time audiovisual feedback to correct your form, just like a coach would.

How It Works

Real-Time Feedback

The system detects incorrect hand rotation or posture and shows you exactly how to fix it, turning practice into perfect form.

Immersive Environments

Row through serene, inspiring environments like Kerala's backwaters, designed to motivate and calm.

Competitive Edge

Race against AI opponents and track your performance with a time-based scoring system to push your limits.

Technical Insight & Learnings

✋

Ghost Overlays

Visual guides with tactile feedback drastically improved user alignment and technique.

🔊

Audio Feedback

Real-time sound cues helped users maintain a consistent, effective rhythm.

🌊

Physics Simulation

Overcoming the challenge of simulating realistic boat control and water drag was key to immersion.

The Next Lap

Row Beyond opens the door to an Adaptive AI Coach, therapeutic calm modes, multiplayer races, and integration with physical rowing machines like Concept2. For rowers who can't easily get to calm open waters, Row Beyond is an easy-to-carry option that boosts their ability and opens up the sport to everyone.
