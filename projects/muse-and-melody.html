Muse and Melody

Jan, 2025 · 4 Weeks
person Solo Project

Step into a cave island where sculptures hold a symphony

Womanhood as a Living Symphony

This VR experience reimagines womanhood as an evolving, multifaceted symphony. It invites you into a hauntingly beautiful cave island where you don't just observe, but actively conduct the light and sound that brings three sculptural muses to life.

"Women exist in a constant interplay of youth, passion, and nurturing spirit. This project is a space to explore that beautiful complexity."

Unlock the Symphony

Interaction 1: The Key

Your journey begins by finding a key to unlock the museum, setting the stage for discovery.

Interaction 2: The Light

Motion-sensing torches illuminate the path, revealing the sacred space step-by-step.

Interaction 3: The Activation

A touch-activated handprint awakens the magic circles behind each muse, signaling their readiness.

Interaction 4: The Conductor

The final act: grabbing a crystal to trigger the full musical score, transforming the space into a complete sensory symphony.

Crafted in Unreal Engine

As my first VR project, this was a deep dive into Unreal Engine. I focused on blueprint scripting for the interactive grabbable objects, trigger volumes, and spatial audio to create a seamless and magical user journey.

More Than a Museum

Muse & Melody taught me that immersion is about agency. By making the visitor the conductor of the experience, the story becomes personal and profoundly more impactful. This project marks the beginning of my journey into meaningful immersive storytelling.
